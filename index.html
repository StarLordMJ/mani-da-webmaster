Who we are

IEEE Student Branch of General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University was established in 2016 as a student branch of the mother body. Since then, we have been volunteering and carrying out so many events to provide facilities to students in the fields of Computer Science, Engineering, Telecommunication and Robotics. We are here to provide you with the best facilities and opportunities to enhance your knowledge and skills in the field of technology.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

    General Information

  • What is the IEEE Student Branch at General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University?

    The IEEE Student Branch is a student-led organization affiliated with the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), the world's largest professional association for advancing technology for humanity. Our branch provides a platform for students interested in electrical engineering, electronics, computer science, and related fields to connect, learn, and develop their professional skills.

  • Who can join the IEEE Student Branch?

    All students at General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University with an interest in engineering, technology, or related fields are welcome to join the IEEE Student Branch. Membership is open to both undergraduate and postgraduate students.

  • What are the benefits of joining the IEEE Student Branch?

    By joining the IEEE Student Branch, you gain access to a wide range of benefits, including:

    • Networking opportunities with fellow students, industry professionals, and IEEE members worldwide.
    • Professional development workshops, seminars, and guest lectures.
    • Participation in technical competitions, conferences, and hackathons.
    • Access to IEEE publications, online resources, and discounts on IEEE products and services.
    • Opportunities for leadership and teamwork through involvement in branch activities.

    • Membership

  • How do I join the IEEE Student Branch?

    To join the IEEE Student Branch, please visit the IEEE website (https://www.ieee.org/) and create a free student membership account. Once you have created an account, you can search for the "General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University" student branch and apply for membership.

  • Are there any fees associated with joining the IEEE Student Branch?

    Yes, there is an annual membership fee for IEEE student members. The exact fee amount may vary depending on your student status and region. You can find more information about membership fees on the IEEE website.

    • Activities and Events

  • What kind of activities and events does the IEEE Student Branch hold?

    The IEEE Student Branch holds a variety of activities and events throughout the year, including:

    • Technical workshops and seminars on various engineering and technology topics.
    • Guest lectures and seminars by industry professionals and academic experts.
    • Field trips to local companies and research institutions.
    • Participation in technical competitions, hackathons, and design challenges.
    • Networking events, social gatherings, and community service activities.

  • How can I stay informed about IEEE Student Branch activities and events?

    You can stay informed about IEEE Student Branch activities and events by following us on social media (if applicable), checking our website regularly, or attending our general body meetings.

    • Additional Information

  • How can I get involved in the IEEE Student Branch?

    There are many ways to get involved in the IEEE Student Branch. You can attend our events, volunteer for our activities, join a committee, or run for a leadership position. We encourage all members to actively participate in the branch and contribute to its success.

  • Who can I contact for more information?

    For more information about the IEEE Student Branch at General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University, please contact us at ieeesbkdu@kdu.ac.lk .
